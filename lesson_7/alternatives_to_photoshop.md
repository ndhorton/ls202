Alternatives to Photoshop:

* GIMP --- GIMP 3 has just come out, with a better UI, increased compatability with PSD files and greater support for non-destructive editing
* Inkscape --- vector graphics editor, aimed at designers, good support for layers
* Scribus
* Blender
* RawTherapee --- good non-destructive editing support
* PhotoGIMP --- Photoshop-like UI overlay for GIMP

* Krita --- primarily aimed at digital artists, photo editing less intuitive
* Photopea --- ads in the free version, some processing features subscription-only, web
* Pixlr --- powerful but primarily for artists, PS-like features uninituitive
* Canva --- primarily for generating designs, some PS-like functionality
* Darktable --- primarily for digital photographers, some PS functionality
* Sumo Paint --- many functions paid-only, mainly for painting and pixel editing
* Paint.NET --- Windows only
